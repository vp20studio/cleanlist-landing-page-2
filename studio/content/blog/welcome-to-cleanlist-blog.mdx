## Why Data Quality Matters

Here's a number that keeps growth teams up at night: **30%**.

That's the average bounce rate for B2B email campaigns using unverified data. Every bounced email hurts your domain reputation, wastes your SDRs' time, and leaves money on the table.

<Callout type="info" title="The Math">
If your team sends 1,000 emails per week with a 30% bounce rate, that's 300 wasted touches—and potentially a damaged sender reputation that affects your entire domain.
</Callout>

Clean data isn't just nice to have. It's the foundation of every successful outbound motion.
